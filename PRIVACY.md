# Privacy Policy — Site Spell Checker

*Last updated: April 1, 2026*

## Overview

Site Spell Checker is a Chrome extension that checks the spelling of text on web pages you visit. All processing happens locally in your browser using a bundled dictionary. No page content is ever transmitted to any server.

## Data We Collect

**We collect nothing.** This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, browsing history, or page content.

## How It Works

When you click "Scan This Page," the extension reads the visible text of the current tab, checks each word against a locally bundled Hunspell dictionary, and highlights potential misspellings directly in your browser. All of this happens entirely on your device.

Spell-check results are cached locally in your browser's session storage for up to 5 minutes so that re-opening the popup is instant. This data never leaves your device and is cleared when you close the browser.

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why it's needed |
|---|---|
| `activeTab` | Temporarily access the current tab only after you open the extension and start a scan |
| `scripting` | Inject the spell-check scanner into the current tab only when you request a scan |
| `storage` | Cache spell-check results locally for 5 minutes |

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not make any automatic network requests. The only outbound connection is the optional **"Scan Your Entire Site"** button, which opens [sitespellchecker.com](https://sitespellchecker.com) in a new tab when you click it. No data is sent automatically.
